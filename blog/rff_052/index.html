Installation Guide

The RFF 052 and RFF 054 setup guide is a printed document provided to the customer with the product. The setup guide instructs installers only on how to fit the device onto rack-mounted devices. It is normally printed on a cardstock card.

My responsibilities were to work with collaborate with Research and Development (Product Development) and technical illustrators to generate text and images for a technical US audience. I wrote the text and instructed the illustrators on what images needed to demonstrate through parsing engineering documents and using the device. However, the styles and layout were designed by the department supervisor.

Programs used:

  • Adobe InDesign
  • Adobe Illustrator
