Setup Guide

The XTP T FB 202 setup guide is a printed document provided to the customer with the device. The setup guide instructs installers only on how to fit the device into several common floor boxes. It is normally printed on a single sheet of paper, folded in half.

My responsibilities were to work with collaborate with Research and Development (Product Development) and technical illustrators to generate text and images for a technical US audience. I wrote the text, organized the topics, and instructed the illustrators on what images needed to demonstrate through parsing engineering documents and using the device. However, the styles and layout were designed by the department supervisor.

Programs used:

  • Adobe InDesign
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
