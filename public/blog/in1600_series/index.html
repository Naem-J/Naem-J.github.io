User Guide

The IN1606 and IN1608 Series user guide is a document provided online by the company to provide extensive configuration and operation information to users. It is normally provided in PDF format.

My responsibilities were to work with collaborate with Research and Development (Product Development) and technical illustrators to generate text and images for a technical US audience. I wrote the text, organized the topics, and captured user interface examples through parsing engineering documents and using the device. However, the styles and layout were designed by the department supervisor.

Programs used:

  • Adobe InDesign
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
