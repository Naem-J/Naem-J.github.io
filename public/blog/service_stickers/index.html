Service Labels

The regional service labels are placed on company products to promote branding and remind users of service contact information. A label for each region (in different languages) was created and maintained in XML files and loaded into Adobe Illustrator templates.

My responsibilities were to design the labels, create templates for different requirements, and maintain XML files of region-specific information. Translations and contact information was provided by local representatives.

Programs used:

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Dreamweaver
